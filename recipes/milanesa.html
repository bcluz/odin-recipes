Beef Milanesa

Description

A delicious dinner recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lb (900g) beef round, thinly sliced 1 cm thick/ 2/5-inch thick
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups (250g) breadcrumbs
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 3 tbsp parsley, chopped
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Slice the beef into a centimeter thick cutlets. Cut off all the fat and the sinew to get as clean of as cutlets as possible.
  2. Tenderize the cutlets with a mallet until half a centimeter thick.
  3. Whisk the eggs with chopped garlic, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drench the cutlets in the egg mixture, then let them soak in it in the refrigerator for 45 minutes.
  5. Heat the oil over medium-high heat until it reaches 170°C/340°F.
  6. Coat the cutlets in breadcrumbs and fry them on both sides for a few minutes, then place them on paper towels to drain.
  7. Serve warm
